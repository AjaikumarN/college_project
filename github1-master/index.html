🏛️ About the Institution

Karunya Institute of Technology and Sciences (Deemed to be University) is a globally recognized private Christian institution, providing technical education combined with spiritual and moral values. It was founded by the late evangelist Dr. D.G.S. Dhinakaran with the vision of raising godly leaders who will serve humanity with compassion and integrity.

The campus is located in a scenic valley surrounded by the Western Ghats and spans over 700 acres. Karunya offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral programs in various disciplines including Engineering, Management, Science, and Arts.

🎯 Vision

To be a center of excellence in education, research, and service to humanity with a Christ-centered mission.

🎓 Mission

  • To create industry-ready graduates with global competencies.
