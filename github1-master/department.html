Department of Computer Science (Shift–II)

📘 About the Department

The Department of Computer Science (Shift–II) at Karunya Institute is dedicated to offering high-quality education with a blend of academic theory, hands-on practice, and values. Established to meet the growing demand in the software industry, the department provides well-structured UG and PG programs in areas like Artificial Intelligence, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, and Full-Stack Development.

With state-of-the-art laboratories, certified faculty, research initiatives, and industry partnerships, the department ensures a futuristic and practical learning experience for students.

🎯 Vision

To produce industry-ready Computer Science graduates with moral values, leadership skills, and innovation mindset.

🚀 Mission

  • Offer curriculum aligned with industry and global standards.
  • Enhance knowledge through research and innovation labs.
  • Organize national-level tech fests, workshops, and guest lectures.
  • Support students in placements, internships, and entrepreneurship.

🏆 Activities & Events

  • XPLORE: State-level Hackathon and Coding Challenge
  • AI & ML Workshop Series with TCS iON
  • Cloud Fest in collaboration with AWS Academy

📈 Achievements

The department has produced several university rank holders and over 1000 alumni currently working in leading IT firms worldwide. Annual placement rate has consistently remained above 90% for the past 5 years.
