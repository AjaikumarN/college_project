✝️ Founder's Vision

Dr. D.G.S. Dhinakaran

Dr. D.G.S. Dhinakaran, the visionary behind Karunya, founded this institution with the dream of providing value-based education for the poor and underprivileged. His faith-driven mission emphasized excellence with compassion.

Today, Karunya continues to fulfill that vision by raising up graduates who are academically sound, ethically strong, and socially responsible.

🎓 Principal's Message

Dr. S. Amutha

At Karunya, we believe education should inspire innovation, character, and a sense of purpose. We nurture students through a personalized and inclusive approach, combining advanced pedagogy with spiritual values.

Our goal is to ensure holistic development and enable students to become global change-makers.

👩‍🏫 HOD’s Message – CS (Shift–II)

The Department of Computer Science (Shift–II) is focused on shaping the next generation of IT leaders. With modern infrastructure, dedicated staff, and vibrant student communities, we emphasize on hands-on learning and real-world problem solving.

We prepare our students not just for jobs, but for lifelong success with integrity, leadership, and innovation.
